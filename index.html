About Me

I am Priyamraj Singh Sisodia, a B.Tech student in Computer Science at the Jodhpur Institute of Engineering and Technology. My technical expertise spans various programming languages and technologies including Python, Flask, Machine Learning, JavaScript, React, and MySQL. I have applied my skills to develop sophisticated projects such as a movie recommendation system, an attendance system, and a breast cancer detection model using advanced machine learning techniques. My professional journey includes a research trainee role at Zeetron Networks, along with conducting workshops and events at my institute, reflecting my commitment to fostering technical knowledge among my peers.
